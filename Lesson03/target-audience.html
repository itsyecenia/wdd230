Target Audience

The target audience for this site would be professionals, community members and businesses interested in becoming involved in the community.

Personas

Kenia

Occupation: Small Business Owner

Kenia is a small business owner in her late thirties interested in expanding her business into one of the locations available in the city. She needs to access the site to find information regarding the proper documentation to open her business according to city code. The site needs to be easy to navigate and simple to search as this is the first time she is looking into expanding her business and is overwhelmed with all that is required.

Mario Martinez

Occupation: Farmer

Mario is a long time farmer in his fifties who wants to offer community classes to inform the community on how to grow your own vegetation. He wants tailor his teachings to the Hispanic community. He wants to be able to access community information. Mario is an older person who is inexperienced using the internet. Information needs to be easy to find.

Scenarios

Cynthia: Cynthia is a community member interested in finding what events are currently going throughout the city.

Mark: Mark is a history professor visiting the city and wants to find all the important landmarks of the city.

Tyler: Tyler is a business man wanted to open his business in the city but is looking for information about the general population. He needs this information to determine whether his business will be successful in the city.

Mary: Mary is trying to figure out where the city goverment buildings are located and what services are offered in each building.

Ted: Ted is a local business owner who is trying to connect to the Chamber of Commerce to try and network with other community members to help increase the cities economic growth.
